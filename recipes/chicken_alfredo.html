Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a decadent and indulgent Italian-inspired dish that embodies comfort and luxury in every bite. It begins with tender pieces of succulent chicken breast, seasoned to perfection and seared to golden perfection. The star of the show, however, is the creamy Alfredo sauce, a velvety blend of heavy cream, butter, and Parmesan cheese, infused with the delicate essence of garlic and fragrant herbs. This luxurious sauce envelops the al dente strands of fettuccine pasta, ensuring each forkful is coated in a rich, velvety blanket of flavor. The chicken adds a hearty protein element, complementing the creaminess of the sauce with its savory tenderness. Garnished with a sprinkle of freshly grated Parmesan cheese and a dash of parsley for color, Chicken Alfredo is a timeless classic that never fails to impress. Its creamy texture and indulgent flavors make it the ultimate comfort food, perfect for a cozy night in or a special occasion dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound uncooked fettucine or linguine
  • 2 boneless chicken breast halves, cooled and cubed
  • 1 jar (16 ounces) Alfredo-style pasta sauce
  • 1 package (10 ounces) frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 can (4.5 ounces) sliced mushroom
  • 1/3 cup of milk

Directions

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Cook fettuccine at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain well.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, place cubed cooked chicken, Alfredo sauce, frozen vegetables, mushrooms, and milk in a large saucepan over medium-low heat. Cook and stir until chicken is heated through and vegetables are tender.
  3. Serve warm Alfredo and chicken sauce over cooked noodles.
